# Game Rules — Space Base

## Overview

Space Base is a dice-rolling, card-buying engine-builder for 2-5 players. Players build a fleet of ships across 12 sectors. On each turn, all players gain rewards based on the dice roll — the active player gains from their stationed ships, while passive players gain from their deployed ships. First player to 40 VP triggers game end.

## Resources

| Resource | Tracker | Starting Value |
|---|---|---|
| Money | Yellow marker | 5 |
| Income | Green marker | 0 |
| Victory Points (VP) | Blue marker | 0 |

---

## Setup

1. Set out all 12 Colony Cards and 6 Cards from each Level (18 Ship Cards visible in Shipyard).
2. Each player places their 12 starting Ship Cards in their correct Sectors (sector number shown top-right of card) on their Console Board.
3. Set Money to 5, Income and VP to 0.
4. Each player draws 1 random Level 1 Card and pays its cost (top-left of card). The replaced Ship Card is **deployed** — turned 180° and placed under the Console Board in the same Sector so the red portion is visible.
5. The player who drew the highest Sector Card goes first (tie: roll 1 die). They take the Start Card.

### Starting Bonuses
| Position | Bonus |
|---|---|
| 1st player | Nothing |
| 2nd player | +1 Money |
| 3rd player | +2 Money |
| 4th & 5th player | +1 Income |

## Card Types

### Ship Cards
- Placed in a specific Sector (1–12) on the Console Board.
- **Blue (Station) reward** — earned by the Active player when they allocate dice to that sector.
- **Red (Deployed) reward** — earned by Passive players when they allocate dice to that sector.
- When a new Ship Card is purchased for a sector, the current card is deployed (moved face-down under the board). Multiple deployed cards stack.

### Colony Cards
- 12 available, not refilled when purchased.
- Score VP immediately on purchase.
- The sector no longer earns Active rewards once a Colony Card is placed there.
- Cannot be replaced.

---

## Turn Structure

### 1. Roll Dice
- Roll 2 six-sided dice.
- May use **Set Dice** (choose both values before rolling) or **Reroll Dice** activations here.

### 2. Allocate Dice
Choose one of:
- **Split**: Use each die separately to activate two different sectors.
- **Combine**: Add both dice together and activate one sector with the sum.
- **Doubles**: If both dice match, may activate that sector twice.

**Active player**: Activates the Blue box in chosen sector(s) and gains rewards. May also use any number of Charged Blue/Green activation boxes.

**Passive players**: Use the same dice result (their own choice of split or combine) to activate their Red Deployed Ship Cards. Take ALL rewards shown. May also use Charged Red/Green activation boxes.

If timing matters: Active player activates first, then each Passive player in turn order.

### 3. Purchase (Optional)
- May buy **1** Ship Card from the 18 face-up Shipyard cards, OR **1** Colony Card.
- Move Money Cube to 0 after any purchase.
- Deploy the current Ship Card in the matching sector (stack behind any existing deployed cards).
- If not buying, do not move the Money Cube.

### 4. End of Turn
- Refill the Shipyard from the matching level deck (Colony Cards are not refilled).
- If Money < Income, move Money up to match Income.
- Pass to the next player clockwise.

---

## Game End

- Triggered when any player reaches **40 VP**.
- Finish the current round so every player has had an equal number of turns from the Start player.
- Player with the most VP wins.
- **Tie**: Play 1 more full round. Repeat until one player has more VP than all others.

---

## Activations & Charges

Some cards have abilities that require Charge Cubes to activate.

- When a card is activated, place 1 Charge Cube in an available box on that card.
- To use the activation ability, remove the required Charge Cube(s) — this is independent of the dice result.
- Cards with multiple linked squares cannot be activated until all squares are filled.
- Spaces with **pips** only need to be filled if the pip count equals the number of players.
- You may use an activation and then recharge it with the dice you allocated this turn.
- If you deploy a card that has Charge Cubes, you may transfer those cubes to the passive side.

---

## Special Mechanics

### 2 Dice + Arrows
- Only usable when combining dice.
- Gain the reward 1 space to the right (2 spaces if double arrow).
- Arrows can chain together, but each reward can only be received once per turn.

### 2X
- After allocating dice and claiming normal rewards, claim 1 activated sector's reward a second time.

## Special Card Abilities

| Ability | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Buy a Card** | Buy the shown number of cards before or after rolling. Does not count as your 1 buy per turn. |
| **Claim a Card** | Take the indicated card for free. |
| **Swap Sectors** | Exchange all cards (including deployed) between two sectors. |
| **Reroll Dice** | Use before any player allocates the dice. |
| **Place 1 Charge Anywhere** | Place 1 Charge Cube on any other Charge Cube space. |
| **Place a Card in 7–12** | Place 1 card regardless of printed sector into any sector 7–12. |
| **Set Dice** | Activate before rolling. You choose the values of both dice. |
| **Exchange This Card** | Switch this card with any other card in a similar position (Station or Deployed) on your Console Board. |
| **You Win!** | Activate this ability to win immediately. |
